這裡，不只是小說。 有歷史深處的嘆息，有人性幽暗中的微光，也有亂世裡依然不肯熄滅的真情與理想。 本欄目收錄野山鹿鳴創作的各類小說作品，包括微型小說、中篇小說、歷史小說與人物故事。有的來自現實人生，有的來自歷史長河，有的來自作者對人性、命運與時代的長久思考。 這裡既有溫柔，也有殘酷；既有繁華如夢，也有黑暗深淵。 願讀者能在故事之中，看見人間。

Fiction Garden小說天地

Here, there is more than fiction. There are sighs rising from the depths of history, faint glimmers of light within the darkness of human nature, and true feeling and ideals that refuse to die even in troubled times. This section collects the various literary works of Yeshan Luming, including microfiction, novellas, historical fiction, and character stories. Some are drawn from real life, some from the long river of history, and some from the author’s enduring reflections on human nature, fate, and the age in which we live. Here, there is tenderness, but also cruelty; splendor like a dream, but also the abyss of darkness. May readers, within these stories, come to see the human world.
